Follow the journey of **ChatGPT & Gemini Floating Messenger**.

## v1.0.0 (Initial Release)
**Released:** March 2026

Chat with ChatGPT & Gemini anywhere, effortlessly.

### Major Features
- **Summon AI on ANY Webpage:** A sleek, non-intrusive chat bubble that floats over your current work tab.
- **Dual AI Mode:** Run ChatGPT and Gemini side-by-side to compare answers and get the best insights.
- **Instant Tab Navigation:** Click `[←]` to jump to the native AI tab, or `[→]` to snap back to your work.
- **Real-Time Sync:** The floating bubble syncs seamlessly with your active AI sessions.

### Developer-Friendly UI
- **Markdown Support:** Enjoy perfectly rendered bold text, lists, and syntax-highlighted code blocks right inside the floating window.

### Privacy & Security
- **No API Keys Required:** Connects securely to your active, logged-in browser sessions.
- **Local Processing:** We do not store, track, or collect your chat data. Everything stays local on your browser.
