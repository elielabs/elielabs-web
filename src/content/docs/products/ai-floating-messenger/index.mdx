## The "Deep Work" Workflow

Whether you are writing code, researching articles, or drafting emails, your AI assistant is now just a click away.

<Steps>

1.  **Install the Extension**
    Add the floating messenger to your Chrome browser.
2.  **Log In**
    Log in to `chatgpt.com` or `gemini.google.com` in your browser.
3.  **Browse Anywhere**
    Go to any website where you need to do deep work.
4.  **Let the Magic Happen**
    Click the floating icon to ask questions, draft text, or compare answers instantly.

</Steps>
