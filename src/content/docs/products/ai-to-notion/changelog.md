Follow the journey of **ChatGPT & Gemini to Notion Clipper**.

## v1.0.0 (Initial Release)
**Released:** January 12, 2026

The smartest way to save AI conversations is here.

### Major Features
- **Smart Context Capture:** Intelligently captures the conversation thread.
- **Auto-Titling:** Uses the user prompt/question as the page title automatically.
- **Zero Context Switching:** Save without leaving the chat tab.

### Formatting Support
- **Tables:** Preserves structure and readability.
- **Code Blocks:** Keeps syntax highlighting intact.
- **Math Formulas:** Renders LaTeX correctly in Notion.
- **Images:** Captures visuals generated by the AI.

### Privacy
- **Local Processing:** Data flows directly from Browser to Notion API. No middleman servers.
