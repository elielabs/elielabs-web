Follow the journey of **Notion AI Bridge: Export & Prompt** (formerly ChatGPT & Gemini to Notion Clipper).[cite: 1]

## v2.0.0 (The PRO Update)
**Released:** April 2026

The ultimate two-way sync is here. We've completely upgraded the engine to bridge your workflows seamlessly.

### Major Updates
- **Complete Rebranding:** Officially transitioned to **Notion AI Bridge: Export & Prompt** to reflect our comprehensive two-way sync ecosystem and expanded capabilities.
- **2-Way Sync:** Added capabilities to **Export Notes** to Notion and **Import Prompts** directly from Notion.
- **PRO Tier Introduced:** Unleash your full potential with advanced features and cross-platform support.

### PRO Features Added
- **Multi-Platform Support:** Added full clipping capabilities for Claude, Perplexity, and DeepSeek.
- **Export Full History:** Added the ability to hold the export button to auto-scroll and capture the entire conversation.
- **Variable Templates:** Introduced `{{variables}}` support for dynamic prompt modularization.
- **Power User Shortcuts:** Added slash commands (`/e`, `/e@all`, `/p`) and custom tagging functionality (`/e:tag`).
- **AI Title Summary:** Integrated local on-device Gemini Nano to generate concise page titles automatically.

## v1.0.0 (Initial Release)
**Released:** January 12, 2026[cite: 1]

The smartest way to save AI conversations is here.[cite: 1]

### Major Features
- **Smart Context Capture:** Intelligently captures the conversation thread.[cite: 1]
- **Auto-Titling:** Uses the user prompt/question as the page title automatically.[cite: 1]
- **Zero Context Switching:** Save without leaving the chat tab.[cite: 1]

### Formatting Support
- **Tables:** Preserves structure and readability.[cite: 1]
- **Code Blocks:** Keeps syntax highlighting intact.[cite: 1]
- **Math Formulas:** Renders LaTeX correctly in Notion.[cite: 1]
- **Images:** Captures visuals generated by the AI.[cite: 1]

### Privacy
- **Local Processing:** Data flows directly from Browser to Notion API. No middleman servers.[cite: 1]
