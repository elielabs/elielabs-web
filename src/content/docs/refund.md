**Last Updated:** April 17, 2026

Due to the digital nature of our products (Chrome Extensions and Notion Templates), all sales are generally final. 

## 1. One-Time Purchases & Templates
Once a license key is issued or a Notion template is duplicated, the product is considered delivered and consumed. Therefore, we do not offer refunds for these items.

## 2. Subscriptions
You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. We do not provide prorated refunds for partial months.

## 3. Technical Guarantee
We stand behind our engineering. If our extension experiences a critical bug that prevents it from functioning as advertised, and our support team cannot provide a workaround or fix within 7 days of your report, we will issue a full refund. 

To request a technical refund, please send us a message via our **[Contact Form](/#contact)** within 14 days of your purchase. Please ensure you include your license key and a detailed description of the issue in your message.
